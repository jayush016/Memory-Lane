# Memory Lane - Video Submission Script (Screen Recording Guide)

**Theme:** Learn Your Roots, Honor Your Elders  
**Tone:** Profound, Inspiring, Emotional, and Technological  
**Total Duration:** ~2:00  
**Method:** Single continuous screen recording.

---

### [0:00 - 0:20] THE VISION: UNITING GENERATIONS
**Action:** 
1. Start on the **Landing Page**. 
2. Immediately click the **"Explore Family Tree"** button.
3. Slowly move the mouse from **Grandma Margaret (Passed)** to **Grandpa Robert (Living)** and then down to **You**.
4. Hover over **Grandpa Robert** to show he is active/living.

**Voiceover:** 
"Look at this family. Three generations. Connected by blood, yet separated by time, distance, and silence. Grandma is gone. But Grandpa Robert is still here—alone in his house, full of stories he thinks no one wants to hear. We built Memory Lane to fix this. To unite the living and honor the passed."

### [0:20 - 0:40] THE BRIDGE: RECORDING HISTORY
**Action:**
1. Click **"Back"** to return to Landing.
2. Click the large **"Share a Memory"** button.
3. Click the **Microphone Icon**. Let the visualizer wave for 3 seconds.
4. Click **"Stop"**.

**Voiceover:** 
"It starts with a voice. For living elders like Grandpa, this is their stage. No typing. He simply speaks. Gemini 3 Pro captures his laugh, his pause, his emotion. It tells him: 'Your story matters. We are listening.'"

### [0:40 - 1:00] EDUCATION: TURNING MEMORIES INTO LESSONS
**Action:**
1. (App transitions to **Story Display**).
2. Mouse hovers over the **AI Watercolor Illustration**.
3. Mouse moves to the **Context Cards** on the right (Historical Facts).
4. Click the **Play Button** briefly.

**Voiceover:** 
"The app transforms his rambling into a history lesson. Gemini extracts the context—showing the grandchild exactly what the world looked like when Grandpa was young. The high cost of living, the music, the events. Personal memories become powerful education."

### [1:00 - 1:15] THE ARCHIVE: VISUALIZING THE PAST
**Action:**
1. Click the **"Gallery"** icon in the header.
2. Click on the **"Christmas 1989"** album to open it.
3. Scroll slowly through the photos.
4. Click on one photo to open the lightbox.

**Voiceover:** 
"And stories need visuals. In the Shared Gallery, we curate the visual history. From that snowy Christmas in '89 to the family road trips. These aren't just files in a cloud; they are moments, organized and tagged, connecting faces to the stories we just heard."

### [1:15 - 1:35] THE GATHERING: GENERATIONS SPEAKING AS ONE
**Action:**
1. Click **"Back"** or Home.
2. Click the **"Gathering"** button (The Group Chat).
3. Type a question: *"What is the secret to a happy life?"* and hit Send.
4. **CRITICAL:** Wait for responses from **Grandpa Robert (Living)** AND **Grandma Margaret (Archived/AI)**.
5. Mouse over Grandpa's response, then Grandma's.

**Voiceover:** 
"Here is where the family truly unites. In the Family Gathering, distance and death disappear. You ask a question, and Grandpa Robert—sitting miles away—answers alongside the memory of Grandma Margaret. Gemini 3 Pro weaves the living wisdom with the archived legacy. The conversation continues."

### [1:35 - 1:50] THE EMOTIONAL PEAK: LIVING MEMORIALS
**Action:**
1. Click **"Join Video Call"**.
2. Show the grid with **You**, **Living Relatives**, and the **AI Deceased Member** together.
3. Hover over Grandma's animated frame.

**Voiceover:** 
"We even bring them back to the table. Using Google Veo technology, we create living memorials. The circle is whole again. The living feel connected. The passed are remembered."

### [1:50 - 2:00] CLOSING STATEMENT
**Action:**
1. Click **"End Call"**.
2. Return to **Landing Page**.
3. Pause on the logo.

**Voiceover:** 
"Memory Lane. Don't wait until they are gone. Connect with them now. Learn your roots. Honor your elders. United forever."
